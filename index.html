About

I’m a strategy and new-business professional who likes the messy beginning of things — validating a market, finding the first partner, and building the operating discipline to scale what works. Over the last several years I’ve worked at the intersection of corporate strategy and execution: translating leadership priorities into targets, then program-managing the path to results.

At Cipla, I help steer strategic planning and performance management for a large prescription-pharma business — turning granular market data into growth decisions and bringing AI into how the organization sees and acts. Before that, on a group leadership program, I built new businesses from zero to one, led market-entry and corporate-development strategy, and closed B2B partnerships across industrial sectors.

I’m an engineer by training (Shiv Nadar University) and an MBA by trade (MDI Gurgaon), and increasingly an AI-native operator — using tools like this site itself, which is built and maintained through an automated publishing system. I care about clarity, leverage, and shipping.
